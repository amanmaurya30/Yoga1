Yoga Volunteer Membership

Membership

A Yoga Volunteer member need not have any prequalification in yoga. The Yoga Volunteer Membership has a nominal fee of INR 250/- for Indian National (Inclusive of SAARC nationals) and USD 100/- for Foreign Nationals.

Benefits

  • A YVM member would be provided a Unique ID and an ID Card.
  • A YVM member is entitled to a free copy of e-newsletter Yogavani.
  • A YVM can join the FREE Online Gurukulam, an interaction with the Gurus and Acharyas.
  • A YVM will be eligible for discounts on publications of the member institutions as per agreement with member institutions.

Life Membership

Membership

Only by Invitation with a contribution of INR 10,000 for Indian Nationals and USD 250/- for Non Resident Indians.

Benefits

  • A LM would be provided a Unique ID and a Certificate of Membership.
